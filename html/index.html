About Humble Light International School

At HLIS, we believe in nurturing students to excel both academically and spiritually. Our mission is to provide a godly atmosphere where students can thrive in their studies while growing in their faith. We offer a wide range of academic programs, extracurricular activities, and community involvement opportunities to help our students become well-rounded individuals.
